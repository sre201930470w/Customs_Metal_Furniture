Our Vision

As the saying goes, “Home is where the heart is”. It is our vision to help every individual create a home that reflects their soul, not just a material house. 

Through exploring their beloved home with us, more people can discover self-expression and self-discovery.

To inspire home owners with our passion and dedication. Assist you in creating and forging real friendships and understandings with each other. The highest quality work will be produced to the best of our abilities.  

Our Design Approach

Every aspect of the product development process is carefully considered, from design to production to long-term impact, bringing function, aesthetic, and detail together in elegant resolution.  

A Unique Sensibility

The versatility of essential forms is central to our design philosophy. Our approach has evolved over time around six core concepts. Balance, Live, Intuition, Light, Color, and Play are all important aspects to consider.  

The Process & The Team

The process of renovating a house can be intimidating, particularly for first-time home buyers. Our team at SRE Metal Furniture will be very happy to help you through this process by offering as many professional advices and services as possible. 

About

At SRE Metal Funiture, we create chairs, tables, and furnishings for community, work and home. Relationships motivate us: between products and spaces, between spaces and people, and between people and their networks. Everything we do is framed in terms of human values, including our soft, essential design sensibility, direct and personalized service, organizational and corporate policies, and global brand identity. 

We are a fast-growing business that was founded in 2013 and is guided by an integrative, human-centered approach. We've evolved from a furniture manufacturing company to an international design powerhouse, doubling our revenue and adding many employees to the SRE Metal Furniture family. 

We can confidently say that having our own workshop, with our own dedicated team of in-house designers and fabricators who are experts in their field of work, many having honed their skills through decades of experience, is our main advantage for our customers. SRE Metal Furniture's design concepts and customized furniture are simple, functional, and stylishly unique. 

Over 8 years of experience, modern designs, high-quality imported materials, and dedicated factory facilities have resulted in over hundreds of satisfied customers, with many returning customers and referrals spanning generations.
