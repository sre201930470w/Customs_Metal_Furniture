Cabinets

Elaborating on this seemingly obvious use of cabinets, the fact of the matter is that cabinets also offer convenient storage spaces. They’re a clever way of making use of existing spaces, such us under your counter-top and stove, to be mounted on walls and not typically as a standalone feature. This allows a lot of room without giving the feeling of being overwhelmed or looking too crowded.
